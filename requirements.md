## Problem Statement
Rural India suffers from limited access to healthcare facilities. Many patients delay diagnosis because doctors are far away, clinics are overcrowded, and awareness is low. This results in preventable illness and death.

## Proposed Solution
AI Rural Health Guardian is an offline-first AI health assistant that helps villagers detect symptoms early, provides basic medical guidance, and connects them with healthcare resources using voice interaction in local languages.

## Target Users
- Rural villagers
- ASHA workers
- Community clinics
- Government health programs

## Key Features
- Voice-based symptom reporting
- Local language support
- AI symptom analysis
- Early disease detection alerts
- Emergency risk scoring
- Government scheme awareness
- Offline functionality
- Secure patient record storage

## Value Proposition
- Early disease prevention
- Reduced hospital overload
- Affordable rural healthcare access
- Support for national health missions

## Expected Impact
- Saves rural lives
- Improves healthcare awareness
- Scalable across India
- Strengthens public health infrastructure
